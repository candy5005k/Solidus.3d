FAQ

Questions buyers ask before they send files.

Short answers focused on decision points that matter most before quoting or prototyping.

Which service should I choose first?

If you only have a concept, drawing, or old part, start with 3D CAD modeling, 2D to 3D conversion, or reverse engineering. If you already have a model and need an output path, we usually route next into 3D printing, CNC machining, or vacuum casting support.

Do you support manufacturing-ready files?

Yes. We focus on geometry that works in quoting and production conversations, not just nice-looking models. That includes tolerances, part simplification, printability checks, and file exports used by vendors and internal teams.

Can you help with low-volume production or bridge runs?

Yes. Vacuum casting, profile cutting, and rapid prototyping support are useful when you need a bridge between concept work and larger-scale production.

Do you also handle visual or architectural work?

Yes. Architectural 3D modeling and product rendering stay on this page so buyers can find both production-focused and presentation-focused options in one place.
